General information

Schematic symbols:

  • Mr – magic ring
  • Sc – Single crochet
  • Inc — increase —single crochet increase
  • Dec — decrease — single crochet decrease
  • SL ST - Slip stitch
  • Ch – chain
  • BLO Back loop only
  • FLO Front loop only
  • (…) – work all stitches in the same stitch
  • (…) *n – repeat instructions in brackets ... times
  • rnd — round
